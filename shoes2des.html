Product Details

Step into timeless elegance with our Classic Leather Oxford Shoes, a testament to fine craftsmanship and sophisticated style. Perfect for both formal and semi-formal occasions, these shoes are made from high-quality full-grain leather, offering durability and a luxurious texture. The soft, breathable leather lining ensures comfort and prevents foot odor, while the cushioned leather insole provides superior support for all-day wear. Featuring a sturdy leather sole with a rubber insert for enhanced grip, these shoes boast a rich, polished black color with a sleek sheen. The classic almond-shaped toe offers comfort without sacrificing style, and the closed lacing system typical of Oxford shoes ensures a snug, adjustable fit. With a low stacked heel for added stability and fine hand-stitched detailing, these shoes also include discreet perforated patterns on the toe cap and sides, adding a touch of traditional elegance.
